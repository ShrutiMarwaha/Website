Bonjour!

I am a PhD in Systems Biology and Physiology with 2.5 years of industry experience. My research has been focused on applying genomics and mathematical modeling approach to study cancer, especially gastric cancer.

My research projects involves:

  • Drug repurposing: Analyzed high-throughput genomics data to identify key genes and pathways dysregulated in gastric cancer and gastric atrophy. Developed computational pipeline for gene-signature based drug repurposing.

  • Mathematical Modeling: Developed an Ordinary Differential Equation (ODE) model of the host's immune response to H. pylori, with focus on crosstalk between cytokines and sonic hedgehog. The model helped to identify a negative feedback loop and potential oscillatory behavior of the system.
